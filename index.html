Dodger Color Privacy Policy

NOTE

Dodger Color does not independently collect or share any data on the user other than the user’s unique device identifier. However, Dodger Color utilizes the third party SDK ‘Unity Ads’ which does collect and share all of the specified required data types listed below. Unity Ads does not process any data ephemerally. Unity Ads also encrypts all data in transit and does provide the option to request that the user’s data is deleted.

Dodger Color only stores the following settings and variables attached to the user’s unique device identifier:

  • Highscore
  • Cursor Sensitivity
  • Enemy Speed
  • Maximum Enemy Spawn Rate
  • Minimum Enemy Spawn Rate


DATA COLECTION & SHARING

  • It is required that Dodger Color collects the user’s unique device identifier for the purpose of saving and loading data such as the highscore.
  • It is required that the 'Unity Ads' SDK collects and shares the user’s approximate location for the purpose of advertising or marketing, analytics, app functionality, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
  • It is required that the SDK collects and shares the user’s personal identifiers for app functionality.
  • The SDK optionally collects and shares the user’s purchase history for the purpose of advertising or marketing, and analytics.
  • It is required that the SDK collects and shares data on page views and tabs as well as any other activity in the app for the purpose of advertising or marketing, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
  • It is required that the SDK collects and shares app diagnostics for the purpose of app functionality, and analytics.
  • It is required that the SDK collects and shares data on the user’s device and other identifiers for the purpose of advertising or marketing, analytics, app functionality, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.
